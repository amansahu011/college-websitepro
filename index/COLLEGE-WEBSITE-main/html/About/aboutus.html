Alwar, known as the City of Parks, takes pride in its rich heritage and culture. MITRC, Alwar contributes to the vibrant essence of Alwar, Rajasthan. Established in 2007, Modern Institute of Technology & Research Centre (MITRC) is an esteemed Engineering and Management College that empowers students to excel and develop a robust personality. Renowned as one of the premier private engineering and management colleges in Alwar, Rajasthan, MITRC is nestled amidst the tranquil Aravali Hills on Alwar-Delhi-Tijara Highway. The institute offers students cutting-edge infrastructure to enhance their understanding of engineering disciplines. Engineering plays a pivotal role in translating scientific breakthroughs into practical solutions for the betterment of society.
MITRC, Alwar is a magnet for aspiring engineers and management students seeking undergraduate and postgraduate programs such as B.Tech, M.Tech, and MBA. Our alumni have made significant contributions across diverse sectors, leveraging their knowledge to benefit industries, academia, research, and beyond.
MITRC Alwar provides students with unique internship opportunities and training programs focusing on the latest technologies. Our students have garnered prestigious accolades including Gold Medals in academics, Smart India Hackathon Grand Finale Winners, and triumphs in various state and national-level technical events.
Situated in the National Capital Region, our campus seamlessly blends urban accessibility with the natural allure of the Aravali Hills, offering a holistic residential experience with top-notch amenities like hostels, dining facilities, sports infrastructure, and recreational options. What sets us apart from other colleges in Alwar are our commitment to academic excellence, distinguished faculty, industry collaborations, research prospects, modern amenities, alumni accomplishments, robust infrastructure, industry linkages, placement opportunities, disciplined environment, active participation in national and state-level technical competitions, and exceptional performance in exams like GATE and overall student satisfaction.

FUNCTIONAL ORGANIZATION
Established in 2007, MITRC Alwar was founded by the “All India Arya Samajis Society for Advance Education & Research”. The institute is overseen by a board of governors, led by the Chairperson of AIIASER Society.

The formation of the All India Arya Samajis Society for Advance Education & Research in 1999 was driven by a group of influential individuals who recognized the need for a platform that nurtures ideas and talent in line with the rapidly evolving world of science and technology. The society’s members were deeply committed to providing meaningful education, conducting original research, and fostering technological innovation to contribute to the industrial growth of the nation.
With the assurance of exceptional faculty and guidance in a serene and picturesque environment, the founders of MITRC Alwar firmly believe that the students will emerge as successful and skilled professionals, becoming the pride of both MITRC Alwar and India.
The AIASSAER took on the responsibility of spreading knowledge and values in Alwar, a city renowned for its parks and often referred to as “The Singhdwar of Rajasthan.” Alwar is also recognized as the Lion Gate of Rajasthan, the Scotland of Rajasthan, and the Kashmir of East Rajasthan.
Academic standards are upheld by the institute, which consists of professors, associate professors, and nominated members. The administrative responsibilities are overseen by the Society Members and senior officials. Input on policies and goals is provided by the Society Board of Governors and advisory council, which includes stakeholders such as Parents, Alumni, Industry, and Academic Experts.
