DIGNITARIES MESSAGE
OBITUARY

Late. Dharmpal Choudhary

Ex MLA-Mundawar, Alwar

It is truly a challenge for us to come to terms with the fact that our College Chairperson, Mr. Dharampal Choudhary Sir, is no longer with us. His sudden passing on April 19th, 2018, has left his family, MITRC, Mundawar, and the state of Rajasthan in deep mourning. Mr. Choudhary was a highly respected figure in both politics and education. Known for his generosity and kind heart, he served as MLA for Alwar District, Rajasthan, during the 11th, 12th, and 14th Legislative Assembly terms in 2000, 2003, and 2013 respectively. He was also recognized as Bhanwar Jitendra, a former Member of Parliament from Alwar who was elected during the Lok Sabha Elections. Additionally, he held the position of Parliament Secretary (Sansdiya Sachiv) during his tenure as MLA from 2003 to 2008. Over the course of more than eleven years, Mr. Choudhary provided guidance to MITRC as Chairperson, fostering a culture of transparency within the organization. Under his leadership, MITRC flourished, offering opportunities for academic and professional growth to all. His legacy will continue to inspire growth and success at MITRC. May his soul rest in peace.

Chair Person’s Message

Mrs. Bimla Devi

Chairperson- MITRC & AIASSAER

Number: +91 9414063671

E-mail: bimla.chairmanmitrc@gmail.com

In the 21st century, India’s emergence as a formidable economic power is a source of pride for all of us. However, this also highlights the pressing need for quality education. India requires more educational institutions to meet the demand for space and AI-based technology courses. In order to achieve the vision of a developed India under PM Modi’s Vikasit Bharat 2047 Initiative, there is a need for a startup ecosystem and innovation in technical institutes. The government of India, through the Startup India mission, has provided a platform for innovative ideas, connected them to funding channels, and established incubators in educational institutions.
MITRC Alwar stands out as an institute that caters to the demand for working professionals with a strong grasp of science and mathematics in industries such as software, production, construction, and semiconductors. We are committed to providing professional education that combines technical expertise with the right values. Our institute focuses on grooming students to be sensitive to the societal challenges faced by both India and the world at large and equips them with the skills and knowledge to address these challenges professionally.
We aim to develop a strong professional and mental framework in our students, enabling them to evaluate, analyze, and solve complex challenges, and ultimately, create their own success stories. We also encourage our students to innovate and develop new products and solutions to tackle real-life challenges, fostering an entrepreneurial spirit.

Director’s Message

Dr. SK Sharma

Director, MITRC

Number: +91 9413106857

E-mail: director@mitrc.ac.in

Professor S.K Sharma holds the Chair Professor position in the Department of Computer Science Engineering at Pacific University, Udaipur. He previously served as the Professor In-charge of Computer Science Engineering. He holds a B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering, an M.Tech in Information and System Security, an MBA, and a Ph.D. in Mathematics and Computer Science Engineering. With over 26 years of teaching experience, Prof. S.K Sharma specializes in Information Security and has published over 25 research papers in National Journals, 25 in International Journals, and 30 in Conferences. He has also guided more than 15 Research Scholars.
