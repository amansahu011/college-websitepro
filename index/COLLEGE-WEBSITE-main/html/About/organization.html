The organizational structure of MITRC Engineering College typically follows a hierarchical framework that facilitates efficient management and administration. While specific details can vary, here's a generalized outline of how such an institution might be structured:
  • Board of Governors/Trustees :

    • At the top level, there is a Board of Governors of All India Arya Samajis Society chaired by Smt. Bimla Devi .This Board is responsible for overarching decisions and policy-making. Board of Members often include prominent individuals from academia, industry, and the community.

  • Advisory Council :

    • The Advisory Council of MITRC serves a crucial role in providing strategic guidance, industry insights, and overall support to enhance the institution's educational quality and relevance. Members of the Advisory Council are selected based on their expertise, influence, and ability to contribute meaningfully to the college's goals. Industry Leaders, Academicians, Alumini are the members of Advisory Council.

  • Director/Principal :

    • Our Director Professor S.K Sharma is the overall head of the institution. He is responsible for the strategic direction, academic leadership, and general administration of the college.

  • Deans :

    • Different faculties within the college may have their own deans (e.g., Dean of Academics, Dean of Exam, Dean of Student Affairs). Deans oversee academic programs, faculty appointments, conduction of exam, research initiatives, and student affairs within their respective departments.

  • Heads of Departments (HoDs) :

    • Each academic department (e.g., Various Departments like Artificial Intelligence, Civil Engineering, Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Applied Science & Business Admistration within the college.) is typically headed by a Head of Department. HoDs manage the day-to-day operations of their departments, including curriculum development, faculty recruitment, and student supervision, academic excellence, activities to enhance skills, discipline and student performance.

  • Faculty Members :

    • Faculty members are responsible for teaching, research, and academic advising. They contribute to curriculum development, conduct research, and often engage in academic and professional development activities.

  • Administrative Staff :

    • The administrative staff includes professionals who manage various non-academic functions such as admissions, finance, human resources, facilities management, public relations, and student services.

  • Support Staff :

    • Support staff includes personnel involved in maintaining facilities, providing technical support (e.g., laboratory assistants), security, and other operational roles essential for the smooth functioning of the college.

  • Students :

    • Students are an integral part of the organizational structure, although not in a formal administrative sense. They participate in academic activities, student organizations, and contribute to the vibrant campus life.
    This hierarchical structure ensures clear lines of authority and responsibility, facilitating effective management and coordination across different functions within MITRC Engineering College.
