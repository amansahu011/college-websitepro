Vision

To produce well trained professionals and leaders for serving society and world at large through excellence in academics, research, development and innovations.

Mission

  • To provide state of the art facility for academic excellence that caters to regional, national and global challenges.
  • To identify emerging areas of technology that can provide innovative and sustainable solutions to global challenges.
  • To provide an environment of industry oriented research and development.
  • To inculcate core values of professional ethics, team-work, inter personal skills and life-long learning for sustainable development of the society.
