OTHER PRIVATE/NGO SCHEMES
Scholarships provided by NGOs (Non-Governmental Organizations) and private organizations can be diverse and target various groups of students based on different criteria such as academic merit, financial need, specific fields of study, or personal background.

Here are some notable scholarship schemes offered by the Private Organisations/NGOs are:
  • SUMEDHA

    • Sumedha is a voluntary non-profit organization registered under the Rajasthan Society Registration Act 1958 with its office at Jaipur.
    The Principal objective of the organization is to help the talented children of poor families who are deprived of higher education due to lack of resources and because of poverty they have to work at low paid jobs in government/private sector. They are unable to showcase their talent to the society and contribute to the development of the country. These talented children sum up to around two-third of the population of India today. It's very difficult to have a realistic estimate of the decay of human capital.
    Late, Sh. Meetha Lal Mehta (Padma Shree), Former Chief Secretary Rajasthan founded this organisation.

      Eligibility:

    • Students who secure above 75% marks in Higher secondary examinations. (a student with lower percentage may also be considered provided he/she meets the preferential criterion).
    • Students with cumulative family income less than Rs. Two lakh per annum.
    • For students already getting assistance, First Division Marks are essential for continuation of scholarship.

      • Prefrence Criteria:

    • Orphan Students.
    • Students with single parent.
    • BPL students.
    • Percentage Obtained.

      • Documents Required:

    • Bhamashah I.D
    • Aadhaar Card
    • Domicile Certificate
    • Income Declaration Form
    • Fee Receipt
    • Mark sheet of last passed examination
    • Caste Certificate
    • Photo & Signature
    Apply Link: https://www.sumedha.org/

  • RELIANCE FOUNDATION

    • Reliance has been providing scholarships to meritorious students for over 25 years, inspired by Reliance Founder-Chairman Shri Dhirubhai Ambani’s belief that the best way to ensure the nation’s progress is to invest in its youth.

      Eligibility:

    • Students must be enrolled in the first-year full-time undergraduate degree in any stream from a recognised Indian institute.
    • Must have passed class 12 with a minimum of 60% marks.
    • The annual household income should be less than INR 15,00,000 (preference will be given to those students whose family income is less than INR 2,50,000).
    • Open for resident Indian citizens only.

      • Documents Required:

    • Passport-sized photograph.
    • Address Proof (for address ticked as preferred physical correspondence address).
    • Mark sheets of 10th and 12th Class Board Examination.
    • Bonafide Student Certificate of current college/institution of enrollment, or Student ID Card.
    • Family Income Proof.
    • Official Disability Certificate (if applicable).
    Apply Link: https://www.buddy4study.com/page/reliance-foundation-scholarships#scholarships

  • ONGC FOUNDATION

    • ONGC scholarship scheme for meritorious students belonging to General category/SC/ST/OBC is an exclusive scholarship opportunity for students belonging to the marginalized sections of society who are studying in the first year of Engineering, MBBS, MBA, or Master in Geophysics/Geology programme.
    The aim of this scholarship is to provide financial support to the meritorious students belonging to the economically weaker General Category. Selected 500 students will receive a scholarship of INR 48,000 each on an annual basis to enable them to complete their education.

      Eligibility:

    • The annual income of the parents/guardian of the student should be up to Rs 4.5 lakh.
    • The student should be studying regularly under a recognized course in a government/recognized educational institution.
    • Have secured a minimum 60% marks in class 12 for Engineering/MBBS course and a minimum of 60% marks in graduation for PG courses in Geoscience/MBA (Note: The minimum OGPA/CGPA should be 6.0 in 10 points scale of grading system).
    • Age Limit: Not be more than 30 years of age.
    • The student should have citizenship of India.

      • Documents Required:

    • Certified copy of Caste Certificate in Hindi/English. (Vernacular may be provided in extreme cases).
    • Copy of Birth Certificate/Class 10 Mark sheet as proof of age.
    • Copy of Class 12-mark sheet in case of Engineering/MBBS students.
    • Copy of consolidated Graduation mark sheet in case of MBA/Master's in Geology/Geophysics students.
    • Certified copy of annual income certificate of the family in Hindi/English language. (Vernacular may be provided in extreme cases).
    • Bank details of the applicant in the ECS form attested by the bank as prescribed.
    • Copy of PAN card or the tentative date for submission of PAN card copy (in case it is not available).
    • Copy of College/Institution Id.
    • Copy of College/Institution Admission Receipt.
    • Copy of undertaking.
    Apply Link: https://ongcscholar.org/#/fellowshipScheme

  • INDUSIND FOUNDATION SCHOLARSHIP SCHEME

    • IndusInd Foundation is a non-profit charitable trust. It was established with the efforts and good wishes of Shri. Srichand P. Hinduja and friends and supporters of the IndusInd family in the year 1995. Its main objective is to provide financial assistance to senior citizens, women, and to provide scholarships to students who have completed their study up to standard 12th and desire to study further till Graduation. We are providing our services in Pan India.

      Eligibility:

    • The annual income of the parents/guardian of the student should be up to Rs 5 lakh.
    • The student should be studying regularly under a recognized course in a government/recognized educational institution.
    • The student should be a citizen of India.

      • Documents Required:

    • Bhamashah I.D.
    • Aadhaar Card.
    • Domicile Certificate.
    • Income Declaration Form.
    • Fee Receipt.
    • Mark sheet of last passed examination.
    • Photo & Signature.
    Apply Link: http://www.theindusindfoundation.org/aboutus.html

  • VIDHYALAKSHMI LOAN SCHEME

    • Vidya Lakshmi is a first of its kind portal for students seeking Education Loan. This portal has been developed under the guidance of the Department of Financial Services (Ministry of Finance), Department of Higher Education (Ministry of Education), and Indian Banks Association (IBA). The portal has been developed and is being maintained by Protean eGov Technologies Limited (Formerly NSDL eGovernance Infrastructure Limited). Students can view, apply, and track education loan applications to banks anytime, anywhere by accessing the portal.

    Apply Link: https://www.vidyalakshmi.co.in/Students/signup

  • ROLLS-ROYCE SCHOLARSHIP

    • With a mission to encourage women to pursue an education and skill training in STEM courses, Rolls-Royce plans to financially support 150 female students to complete their engineering programme over the next 3 years. Kishore Jayaraman, President of Rolls-Royce, India & South Asia said, “Our STEM outreach programmes are forging exciting connections between classroom studies and STEM in the wider world. Taking this to the next level, we are happy to partner with Buddy4Study and CAF India to enable a scholarship program for women engineering students, and support and to inspire the next generation of engineers, problem-solvers, scientists and innovators.”

      Eligibility:

    • Girl students who are currently studying in the 1st/2nd/3rd year of the engineering degree program (in fields like Aerospace, Marine, Electronics, Computers, etc.) at AICTE-recognized institutions are eligible.
    • The applicants must have scored more than 60% marks in their Class 10 and 12 board exams.
    • Annual family income must not exceed INR 4 lakh per annum.
    • Open for students from Pan India.

      • Documents Required:

    • Passport size photograph.
    • Aadhaar card.
    • Current academic year admission proof [AY 2024-25 (college ID card/admission fees receipt, Bonafide certificate/ admission letter, etc.)].
    • Class 10 and Class 12 marksheet (self-attested copy).
    • Previous academic year marksheet (2023-24).
    • Bank account details of the scholarship applicant (cancelled cheque/passbook copy).
    • Family income proof (any of the three proofs given below):
      • Income Proof issued by Gram Panchayat/Ward Counsellor/Sarpanch.
      • Income Proof issued by SDM/DM/CO/Tehsildar.
      • Salary slip.
    Apply Link: https://www.buddy4study.com/page/rolls-royce-unnati-scholarships-for-women-engineering-students

    • Candidates who have passed 12th standard securing a minimum of 60% in 10th and 12th Std board exams.

      • Documents Required:

    • 1 Photo ID Proof (Any one from list of accepted ID documents):
      • PAN Card

      • 10th std marks card with photo

  • L’ORÉAL INDIA SCHOLARSHIP

    • The L’Oréal India For Young Women in Science (FYWIS) Scholarship programme is a countrywide initiative which encourages and supports young women to pursue their careers in science. By providing them financial assistance to pursue scientific education, this scholarship reinforces the vocation of women in scientific disciplines. The FYWIS scholarship encourages women, who would probably otherwise discontinue their education, to pursue the study in their chosen field.

      Eligibility:

    • Women candidates who have passed Class 12 in Science stream from India in the academic year 2023-24.
    • Must have scored a minimum of 85% in PCM/PCB/PCMB in Class 12.
    • Annual family income of the applicant should be less than INR 6,00,000.
    • Must be taking admission to undergraduate (bachelor’s) programmes in science-related fields in the academic year 2024-25.
    • Students having a one-year gap after Class 12 will not be considered. The final selection would be subject to the submission of the admission letter from a recognized college/university in India.
    • Candidates who have passed 12th standard securing a minimum of 60% in 10th and 12th Std board exams.

      • Documents Required:

    • Attested copy of age proof (Aadhaar Card, Birth Certificate, Passport, Voter ID Card, etc.).
    • Self-attested copy of parents' income proof (any one):
      • Income Certificate issued by a government body
      • Salary slip issued by permanent employer
      • Form 16 (Income-Tax returns form)
    • Mark sheet of Class 10 attested by the school authorities.
    • Mark sheet of Class 12 attested by the school authorities.
    Apply Link: https://www.buddy4study.com/page/loreal-india-for-young-women-in-science-scholarships

  • TATA CAPITAL PANKH SCHOLARSHIP 2024-25

    • This Scholarship program aims to provide financial support to students from economically weak sections who are pursuing higher education. The scholarship, initiated by Tata Capital Limited, offers a one-time grant covering up to 80% of course fees, capped at INR 10,000 to INR 12,000. This program targets students in Classes 11 and 12, as well as those enrolled in general graduate, diploma, or ITI courses. The objective of this Tata Capital Pankh Scholarship 2024-25 is to provide financial assistance to the students pursuing undergraduate or postgraduate degrees in Nursing, MBBS, BDS, PG Medical courses (any specialty), Paramedical courses, ITI/Diploma subjects like Fitter, Electrical, Welder, Safety, etc. Students can receive a grant of INR 1,00,000 for a year, which will help them in their education.

      Eligibility:

    • Students studying in Classes 11 and 12 at a recognized academic institution in India can apply.
    • Candidates must have scored at least 60% marks in the preceding class.
    • The annual family income of the applicants from all sources should be less than or equal to INR 2.5 lakh.

      • Documents Required:

    • Attested copy of age proof (Aadhaar Card, Birth Certificate, Passport, Voter ID Card, etc.).
    • Self-attested copy of parents' income proof (any one):
      • Income Certificate issued by a government body
      • Salary slip issued by permanent employer
      • Form 16 (Income-Tax returns form)
    • Mark sheet of Class 10 attested by the school authorities.
    • Mark sheet of Class 12 attested by the school authorities.
    Apply Link: https://bharti-axagi.co.in/tata-capital-pankh-scholarship-2024-25/
