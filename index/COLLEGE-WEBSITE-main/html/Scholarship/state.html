STATE SCHOLARSHIP SCHEMES
The Government of Rajasthan provides several scholarship schemes to support students across various categories. These schemes are aimed at encouraging education among economically backward and marginalized sections of society.
Major scholarship schemes offered by the Rajasthan Government to support the education of students belonging to various categories. The exact details regarding eligibility criteria, application process, and deadlines can be found on the official website of the Rajasthan Government's Department of Social Justice and Empowerment or through the Rajasthan Scholarship Portal. It's advisable for eligible students to regularly check for updates and apply within the stipulated deadlines to avail of these benefits.
SCHOLARSHIP PORTAL : https://sje.rajasthan.gov.in/Scholarship_Portal.aspx
Here are some notable scholarship schemes offered by the Rajasthan Government:
  • POST MATRIC SCHOLARSHIP-SCHEDULED TRIBES (ST CATEGORY)

    • This scholarship is for students belonging to Scheduled Tribes (ST) studying at post-matriculation or post-secondary stage.In this scheme, as per rules, non-refundable fees and maintenance allowance are included and given in the form of scholarship.

      Eligibility:

    • The annual income of the parents/guardian of the student should be up to Rs 2.50 lakh.
    • The student should be studying regularly under a recognized course in a government/recognized educational institution.
    • The student should belong to Scheduled Caste/Scheduled Tribe.
    • The student should be a native of Rajasthan.

      • Documents Required:

    • Bhamashah I.D.
    • Aadhaar Card
    • Domicile Certificate
    • Income Declaration Form
    • Fee Receipt
    • Mark sheet of last passed examination
    • Caste Certificate
    • Photo & Signature
    Apply Link: https://sje.rajasthan.gov.in/

  • POST MATRIC SCHOLARSHIP-SCHEDULED CASTE (SC CATEGORY)

    • This scholarship is for students belonging to Scheduled Caste (SC) studying at post-matriculation or post-secondary stage. In this scheme, as per rules, non-refundable fees and maintenance allowance are included and given in the form of scholarship

      Eligibility:

    • The annual income of the parents/guardian of the student should be up to Rs 2.50 lakh.
    • The student should be studying regularly under a recognized course in a government/recognized educational institution.
    • The student should belong to Scheduled Caste/Scheduled Tribe.
    • The student should be a native of Rajasthan.

      • Documents Required:

    • Bhamashah I.D.
    • Aadhaar Card
    • Domicile Certificate
    • Income Declaration Form
    • Fee Receipt
    • Mark sheet of last passed examination
    • Caste Certificate
    • Photo & Signature
    Apply Link: https://sje.rajasthan.gov.in/

  • POST MATRIC SCHOLARSHIP-OTHER BACKWARD CASTE (OBC –SBC CATEGORY)

    • This scholarship is for students belonging to SBC-Special Category (OBC-SBC) studying at post-matriculation or post-secondary stage. In this scheme, as per rules, non-refundable fees and maintenance allowance are included and given in the form of scholarship.

      Eligibility:

    • The annual income of the parents/guardian of the student should be up to Rs 1 lakh.
    • The student should be studying regularly under a recognized course in a government/recognized educational institution.
    • The student should belong to Backward Class.
    • The student should be a native of Rajasthan.
    • The student should belong to the 17 categories of Other Backward Classes determined by the department mentioned below:-
    • B.P.L. Daughter of Card Holder
    • B.P.L. Card Holder's Son
    • Daughter of Antyodaya Card Holder
    • Son of Antyodaya Card Holder
    • State BPL Card Holder's Daughter
    • State B.P.L. Card Holder's Son
    • Orphan Girl
    • Orphan Boy
    • Widow Herself
    • Widow's Daughter
    • Widow's Son
    • Divorced Woman herself
    • Daughter of a Divorced Woman
    • Son of a Divorced Woman
    • Specially Abled Person Himself
    • Specially Abled Person's Daughter
    • Son of a Special Person

      • Documents Required:

    • Bhamashah I.D.
    • Aadhaar Card
    • Domicile Certificate
    • Income Declaration Form
    • Fee Receipt
    • Mark sheet of last passed examination
    • Caste Certificate
    • Photo & Signature
    Apply Link: https://sje.rajasthan.gov.in/

  • Dr. AMBEDKAR ECONOMICALLY BACKWARD CLASS POST MATRIC SCHOLARSHIP SCHEME

    • This scholarship is for students belonging to Economically Backward Caste (EBC) studying at post-matriculation or post-secondary stage. In this scheme, as per rules, non-refundable fees and maintenance allowance are included and given in the form of scholarship.

      Eligibility:

    • The annual income of the parents/guardian of the student should be up to Rs 1 lakh.
    • The student should be studying regularly under a recognized course in a government/recognized educational institution.
    • The student should be of general category belonging to Economically Backward Class (other than Scheduled Caste, Scheduled Tribe, Other Backward Class and Special Backward Class) i.e General Category/OBC-Non SBC.
    • The student should be a native of Rajasthan.

      • Documents Required:

    • Bhamashah I.D.
    • Aadhaar Card
    • Domicile Certificate
    • Income Declaration Form
    • Fee Receipt
    • Mark sheet of last passed examination
    • Caste Certificate (For OBC-Non SBC)/EWS Certificate
    • Photo & Signature
    Apply Link: https://sje.rajasthan.gov.in/

  • CHIEF MINISTER SARVAJAN HIGHER EDUCATION SCHOLARSHIP SCHEMEP

    • Students studying in the educational institutions attached with the scheme will be reimbursed only half of the mandatory non-refundable fees/fees charged by the educational institution, i.e. 50 percent of the fee amount. Maintenance allowance and refundable fees will not be reimbursed.

      Eligibility:

    • The annual income of the parents/guardian of the student should be up to Rs 5 lakh.
    • The student should be studying regularly under a recognized course in a government/recognized educational institution.
    • The student should be of general category belonging to any Category (other than Scheduled Caste, Scheduled Tribe, Other Backward Class and Special Backward Class) i.e General Category.
    • The student should be a native of Rajasthan.

      • Documents Required:

    • Bhamashah I.D.
    • Aadhaar Card
    • Domicile Certificate
    • Income Declaration Form
    • Fee Receipt
    • Mark sheet of last passed examination
    • EWS Certificate
    • Photo & Signature
    Apply Link: https://sje.rajasthan.gov.in/

  • LABOR CARD SCHOLARSHIP

    • The residents of Rajasthan state are provided with a Shramik/Labor Card through which the family members of the beneficiaries are able to avail benefits ranging from Rupees 8000 to Rupees 35000. Now, the Government will be providing a scholarship opportunity to the people having this Shramik/Labor Card. You can achieve a lot of different types of scholarship benefits by using your Shramik/Labor Card if you are a resident of the Rajasthan state. Different amounts of scholarship will be provided to the candidates pursuing different educational courses.

      Amount of Scholarship:

    • Rs. 18000 Per Year - For Graduation Professional Course Pursue by Male.
    • Rs. 20000 Per Year - For Graduation Professional Course Pursue by Female.

      Eligibility:

    • The applicant should be a construction worker registered as a beneficiary on the Board.
    • The son/daughter/wife of the beneficiary will be eligible for Education Assistance (Scholarship).
    • Only two children of the applicant or one child and wife will be eligible to receive the scholarship, but if both spouses are registered beneficiaries, a maximum of two children of the spouse will be eligible for the scholarship. But there will be no limit for a cash award to meritorious students.
    • Regularly studying in a private school or college recognized by the Government or Central / State Government in class 6 to postgraduate level, Or Regularly studying in Government or recognized private ITI and polytechnic courses conducted in the state.
    • The meritorious student must have passed the class 8 to 12th examination with 7% marks or equivalent grade to receive the cash award. Have attained/passed 60 percent or more marks or equivalent grades in diploma, undergraduate, and postgraduate level examinations (including medical, engineering, or other professional examination).
    • The beneficiary’s wife should not be more than 35 years of age to be eligible for the scholarship and should be studying regularly in a teaching institution.
    • The scholarship for a year will be payable only after passing the relevant examination.
    • The scholarship will be eligible only if the student gets admission to the upcoming class after the summer vacation. But in the event of passing the final examination of the 12th class, diploma, graduate, or postgraduate course, it will not be necessary to enter the next class.

      • Documents Required:

    • Bhamashah I.D
    • Aadhaar Card
    • Domicile Certificate
    • Income Declaration Form
    • Fee Receipt
    • Mark sheet of last passed examination
    • Photo & Signature
    • Copy of registration labor (Shramik) card of the beneficiary.
    • Copy of the first page of the beneficiary’s bank account passbook (wherein the beneficiary’s name, bank account number, and IFSC code are mentioned).
    • Self-attested copy of the mark sheet of the class or course for which the scholarship is sought.
    • It is necessary to sign and stamp the prescribed column of the form by the head of the teaching/training institution.
    • Last 12 months certificate of being a construction worker of a beneficiary.
    Apply Link: https://labour.rajasthan.gov.in/
