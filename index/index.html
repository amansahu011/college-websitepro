About Us

MITRC Alwar add another feather in the already colorful cap of Alwar, Rajasthan. Since its inception in 2007, Modern Institute of Technology & Research Centre (An Engineering and Management College), Alwar has been known to enable students to carve a niche for themselves and develop a strong personality. Alwar is famous for its Tourism, Dishes, Cuisine, Education Hub, Industries and Jobs.

MITRC Engineering College in Alwar is one of the best engineering colleges in Alwar. MITRC offering various programs i.e. UG & PG in Artificial Intelligence and its specialization, Computer Science Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Civil Engineering and Mechanical Engineering, MITRC has established itself in Year 2007 and prove itself as a leader in technical education that makes it ranked one among top 10 engineering colleges in Alwar. Along with other engineering institutes in Alwar like Institute of Engineering and Technology Alwar, Laxmi Devi Institute of Engineering and Technology Alwar, Sunrise University, and NIET Alwar, MITRC has consistently produced successful graduates who have proved their excellence in various fields and accomplish the demand of society and industries. Thus, Our Alumini Success make MITRC as Top B.Tech Private College in Alwar. The college is known for its impressive placements and has seen students securing positions in top companies. MITRC is affiliated to Bikaner Technical University and Rajasthan Technical University (Old Batches), MITRC ensures that its students are well-prepared for the industry as well as for society. The college has been ranked as one of the top engineering colleges in Alwar and boasts a high cut-off for admissions specially in Computer Science Engineering and its Specialization. For those looking for the best college for engineering in Alwar, MITRC is a top choice. With its excellent faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and strong academic reputation, MITRC Engineering College is truly a top-ranking institute and Best Institute of Engineering and Technology in Alwar. With its commitment to providing quality education and putting students on the path to success, MITRC Engineering College is undoubtedly a top choice for aspiring engineers.
